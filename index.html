electron-pwa-demo

Drag the title to move this window. Alt+F4 is disabled. App supports browser-sync. Ctrl+Shift+X to exit. CTRL+Shift+O to open file dialog, even when outside the app. F12 open debug tools. Or use the tray icon. Launch a modal with an exteral app. Drag those tiles. Double click the title to maximize.

We are using node , Chrome , and Electron .
